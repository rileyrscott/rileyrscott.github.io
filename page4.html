Built in ND filter

The URSA Mini is comes with a built in neutral density filter, which echances image quality within a high light and exposure enviroment. This built in filter provides a way to take care of the once common and unpleasent hassle of buying seperate filters for each lens you own. This is an all in one!.

Low Light

The Ursa mini is built to shoot in even the lowest of light. Despite very harsh dark exposure the Ursa mini is capable of producing a high quality image that ones eyes could not even see imaginable. This is truly a great step in the world of cinema.

Cinematic

The Ursa mini provides the image of a industry standard cinema camera, proving itself with its stunning cinematics. The quality produces work that is seen as powerful and of true cinematic beauty.
