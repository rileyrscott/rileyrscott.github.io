Interchangeable Lens Mounts

The URSA Mini is sold with EF/PL mounts, each having seperate bodies. The ursa is made for a wide varitey of cinema lenses featuring canon, sony, rokinon, and much more. The qualtiy and aspects of these mounts are key to great cinematography.

Custom Controls

The Ursa mini is built with seperate controls to enchance the user experiance. These controls range from panels featuring white balances to manueling adjusting your own shutterspeed and film slate, the possibilities are endless.

Slim Design

The classic ursa mini is built to contain all features of a great cinema camera within a small and portable body. It's sleek body design includes a built in mic and fan. The body is also built to add on seperate featurings, including a quality viewvinder!
